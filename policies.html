Store policies

Plain-language information for our initial catalogue and inquiry-based ordering process.

← Back to the store

Shipping and Kingston pickup

  • Most products are sourced after availability is confirmed and are normally ready within approximately 5–7 business days.
  • Free Kingston pickup is available by appointment after the order is ready. The exact pickup location is shared privately.
  • Canada-wide shipping is available for eligible products after the destination, package size and courier charge are confirmed.
  • Shipping estimates begin after the product has been received or released by our supplier.
  • If an item becomes unavailable before payment, we will offer an alternative or let you cancel the request.

Returns and product concerns

  • Contact us before returning any item. An authorization and return instructions are required.
  • Unused accessories in original condition may be considered for return within 7 days of receipt, subject to supplier acceptance and return-shipping costs.
  • Used, knocked-in, oiled, damaged or custom-selected cricket bats are normally final sale unless there is a verified manufacturing defect.
  • Natural differences in willow grain, shade, weight and small cosmetic marks are not automatically manufacturing defects.
  • Report shipping damage within 24 hours and keep the packaging. Include clear photographs of the item and parcel.
  • Warranty claims are assessed with the supplier or manufacturer and are not guaranteed until approved.

Before publishing: confirm this policy with your supplier and adjust the return window or conditions to match your actual arrangement.

Privacy notice

When you submit the website form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email, phone number, city, selected product and message. We use it to respond to your inquiry, confirm product availability and help arrange an order.

  • Form submissions may be stored in a private Google Sheet and delivered to the business owner by email.
  • We do not sell contact-form information.
  • We retain inquiry details only as reasonably needed for customer service, transaction records and legal obligations.
  • You may contact us to request correction or deletion of inquiry information, subject to records we are required to retain.

Website and ordering terms

  • Prices are displayed in Canadian dollars and may change before an order is confirmed.
  • The website is an inquiry catalogue, not an automatic inventory or checkout system.
  • An order is not accepted until availability, final price, taxes if applicable, shipping and payment are confirmed directly.
  • Product photographs may be representative. Exact bat photographs and weight can be provided when available.
  • Descriptions are provided in good faith using supplier information. Contact us before purchasing if a specification is essential.
  • We may correct pricing, description or availability errors before accepting payment.
